HOW TO DO FLOWER ARRANGEMENT

Learn how to arrange flowers and create a colorful, professional-looking bouquet. Use these flower arranging techniques to beautifully display blooms and keep them looking fresh.

So lets start

While anyone can plop a handful of flowers into a vase, there is a little more thought and technique that goes into the art of flower arranging. It requires a bit of planning and careful preparation to create a beautiful centerpiece and ensure flowers maintain their color and freshness. Instruction

Start by adding the largest flowers first, working in a circle and turning the vase as you go to make sure the arrangement is symmetrical. Layer in the next variety of flower and repeat until all flowers have been added. Finish the arrangement by adding greenery, grasses, or berries.

My Personal Tip

As a rule, make your bouquet about one and a half times taller than its container. You should also balance the width and the height. Remove any thorns or leaves from flowers. This will prevent harmful bacteria from entering the water and reducing their lifespan.
